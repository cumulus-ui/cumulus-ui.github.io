---
title: Badge
description: A small label that displays a status or count.
tag: cs-badge
---

## Usage

```html
<cs-badge color="blue">Active</cs-badge>
<cs-badge color="grey">Inactive</cs-badge>
<cs-badge color="red">Error</cs-badge>
```

## Colors

- `blue` — informational
- `grey` — neutral / inactive
- `red` — error / critical
- `green` — success
